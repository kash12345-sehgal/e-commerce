## 🏗️ System Architecture & Core Features

### 1. Admin Management Dashboard
Designed for store administrators to manage inventory, track metrics, and handle logistics seamlessly.
*   **Inventory Control (CRUD):** Full management of product catalogs, variations, pricing models, and stock levels.
*   **Order Fulfillment:** Comprehensive tracking of customer orders from processing to delivery status.
*   **Data Analytics:** High-level overview of revenue streams, sales performance, and user sign-ups.

### 2. Client-Facing Frontend
A conversion-optimized web application engineered for an optimal customer shopping experience.
*   **Dynamic Product Catalog:** Advanced filtering, sorting, and instantaneous search functionality.
*   **State-Managed Shopping Cart:** Persistent cart management with optimized checkout workflows.
*   **Responsive UI/UX:** Mobile-first architecture ensuring seamless layouts across all device viewports.
